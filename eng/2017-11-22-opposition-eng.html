Recently, a acquaintance asked me if I was opposed to the government. The question made me think, and soon I found an answer that surprised both my interlocutor and myself. I am not opposed to the government - I am opposed to my own people. I see not the government, but our own Ukrainian people as the main enemy. I am in an irreconcilable opposition to them. I consider them to be the cause of all troubles and believe it is necessary to urgently purge them.

In my deep conviction, the main political conflict in Ukraine is not the power-opposition conflict, but the conflict between the intelligent and the ignorant. It is evident that the number of the latter is rapidly increasing.

Both in power and in opposition, there have been wealthy and intelligent people for many years who successfully exploit the foolish and obedient population. The problem is not with them. They are not the root of evil, they are merely its consequence.

Evil is gullibility, greed, political illiteracy, ignorance, thievery, servility, and citizens’ irresponsibility. Such a people rightfully have the power and opposition they have. Ukrainian society needs not parliamentary opposition to the government, but a nationwide opposition to fools.

Here are four categories of “fools” that, in my opinion, are the most dangerous and need to be fought against first and foremost, at all levels and whenever possible:

Recently, I was asked what exactly we, specifically you and I, can do to change the situation in our impoverished and devastated Ukraine. I believe that we should start by fighting against these four categories of our enemies. Fighting at a level accessible to us, in everyday communication, on social media, in offices and at home.

These people are the starting point of our national troubles.

Они — враги.

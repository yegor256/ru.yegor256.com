It’s good that all of this is already in the distant past.

In dark times, runaway peasants were returned to the feudal lord, who both executed and showed mercy. Interestingly, the power of laws suits us, while the power of a specific person causes rejection. We are much more willing to be slaves to the constitution, police, and society than to an individual.
